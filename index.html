Video Game Sales Analysis

Nature of Enterprise – Akaisha Gurung

Project Overview

This project analyses a video game sales dataset containing 16,598 games. The aim was to explore sales trends across genres and regions using descriptive statistics, visual analysis and linear regression.

Linear Regression Model

Global_Sales = 0.0632 + 1.7918 × NA_Sales
R² = 0.8856

The high R² value shows a strong relationship between North American sales and global sales, meaning NA sales are a strong predictor of overall performance.
